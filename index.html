👋 ABOUT ME

Hey, I’m William — a robotics engineer and Ph.D. student designing intelligent systems that move through the world (and sometimes the forest 🌲 ) on their own.

My focus is on real-time autonomy, sensor fusion, and embedded AI, especially for rugged, off-road, and edge-case environments.

I've worked across industry and academia, with engineering and research experience at McGill, Tesla, CTA (BRP), INO, and ABB. Whether I’m writing firmware for robots, training deep learning models, or spinning up a new PCB, I’m always chasing the magic of turning ideas into working systems.

Right now, I’m building modular autonomy frameworks for multi-vehicle platforms, contributing to a startup developing next-gen EV batteries ⚡, and experimenting with how LLMs can control robots out in the wild. If that sounds like your kind of thing, I’d love to chat.
